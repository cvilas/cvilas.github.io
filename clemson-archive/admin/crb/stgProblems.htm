HELP! My ServoToGo board does not work.
Author: Vilas Kumar Chitrakaran, May 2006.

So you have setup a new QNX PC for your experiments in the lab according to the guidelines provided by that guy. You have installed the ServoToGo data acquisition board and the QRTS STG-SDK. Upon running the STGServer program, you notice that the timer chip on the board doesn't seem to be counting, i.e., the timer ticks don't increment in the ServerManager window (on pterm prompt, type sm). *DON'T PANIC*. The following may fix your problem:

  1. Reboot, and enter bios settings.
    • Set the option for 'PnP OS' to 'disabled'. (QNX does not support PnP).
    • Manually assign IRQ 5 and 7 to 'Legacy ISA'. The STG board uses these interrupts.
  2. STG board has a noise related problem. Do as they suggest in their errata page for the fix.
  3. Make sure that the PC power supply is proper ATX compliant. We had a few power supplies with the -5 volts conductor missing (pin 18) from the ATX connector that plugs into the motherboard. If this is not the case, check the signals in all the 20 pins anyway. The ATX connector pinout is given below.
