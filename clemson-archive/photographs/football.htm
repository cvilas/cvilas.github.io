Football @Clemson

Photo 1: The Death Valley roars with approximately 80,000 spectators during the home game with Georgia Tech.

People here are crazy about football, like the way we Indians are crazy about cricket.
Fall is also the football season, and every game is an occasion for celebration.


Photo 2: Football fans camp all over the university when there is a game. Games happen during Saturdays.


Photo3: You better come real early if you need  parking space.

I normally work at the parking lot 7 during these games. It doesn’t get to be as boring as
other student police positions because there are a lot of people around here to talk to,
and this is also one of the most scenic places within the university. I get to spend most
of my time reading or talking to people. It is particularly interesting talking to some of
the old-timers, people who have once been students of this university.


Photo 4: Everybody, the young and the old alike are passionate about the game.


Photo 5:  The Death Valley is filled to capacity during most of the games.
