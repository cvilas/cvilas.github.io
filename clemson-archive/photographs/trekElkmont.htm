Things you will need when you go camping:

  1. Maps to the location.
  2. Enough cash for expenses that cannot be paid for using credit card.
  3. Camping
    • Tents, sleeping pads, sleeping bags, fire-wood and igniter.
    • If possible, reserve campsite before-hand.
  4. Tools
    • Flashlight, knife, Hammer, lighter.
    • alarm clock, two-way radio, pocket radio/cassette player (helps if news updates required).
    • First aid kit (band-aid, aspirin, )
  5. Clothing
    • winter : gloves, jackets, cap, trekking shoes.
    • summer :

Did I miss anything here? Mail me and let me know.

As a group, this was our first trip together, and we had been planning on doing something of this sort for a really long time. But it was a year before Ram could finish cataloging all the exotic insects in the sahara desert (he works on a project for the National Geographic), and sudhir could finish his ground-breaking research on RF transmitter design (it still doesn't work). My research was in a state of coma anyway. So it was the right time for the trip.
