Mission in life
What is the purpose of my life? What am I trying to achieve. Will I have achieved something unique and important before I die? Will my story be worth telling someone. Most of us lead ordinary lives. We plan our lives just enough to lead an ordinary life, and we mostly think only about ourselves - better grades, graduation, and a job that pays us decently. How about having a mission. How about working towards a goal that is worthy of being pursued, even worth dying for. What kind of a mission should that be?
